NeatTube

YouTube Enhancement Settings

Extension

Master switch — disables all features when off.

🚫 Shorts Removal

Hide Shorts in sidebar
Hide Shorts shelves (home / search / subs)
Redirect /shorts/ URLs to /watch

🔒 Filter out Members-Only

Hide members-only badges
Hide members-only shelves / cards

Uses heuristics to detect members-only content. May occasionally miss or over-match.

🖼️ Picture in Picture

Auto PiP (enter automatically on tab switch)

Toggle the active video using the shortcut Alt+P. Works best when video is playing.

👎 Show Dislike count

Uses the Return YouTube Dislike public API. Counts are community-estimated and may not be exact.

🎬 Auto HD Quality

Preferred resolution
Reapply on navigation

Best-effort: YouTube may override based on bandwidth, codec, or stream availability. Falls back to the nearest lower available resolution.

🐛 Debug Mode

Logs module activity to the browser console with [NeatTube] prefix.
